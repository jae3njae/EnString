Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

This policy explains what information EnString collects when you use the app, why, and how it's handled. EnString ("we," "us," or "our") is a personal project that provides an online canvas for organizing ideas. By creating an account and using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

What we collect

When you sign in, we collect the minimum needed to give you an account and save your work:

  • Account details. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. If you sign in with email, we store your email address and a securely hashed password (handled by our authentication provider — we never see your raw password).
  • Your content. The boards you create — notes, pages, images, tables, checklists, links, groups, and connections — are saved so you can return to them.
  • Basic technical data. Standard information needed to operate the service, such as timestamps of when your data was last saved.

We do not collect location data, we do not track you across other websites, and we do not run advertising.

Cookies and local storage

EnString uses your browser's local storage to keep you signed in between visits and to remember preferences such as your chosen theme. These are functional only — we do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. Clearing your browser storage will sign you out but will not affect boards already saved to your account.

How your data is stored

Accounts and board content are stored using Supabase, a hosted database and authentication service, on servers located in the United States. Each account's data is protected by row-level security, meaning your boards can only be read or changed by your own logged-in account. The app itself is served as static files from GitHub Pages.

Security

We rely on established providers and take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and database access rules that isolate each account's data. No online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we work to keep your information protected and to limit access to it.

How we use your data

  • To sign you in and keep you signed in.
  • To save, load, and display the boards you create.
  • To operate, maintain, and secure the service.
  • To respond to you if you contact us for support.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties except the service providers that make the app run (our authentication and database provider, and our static host), and where required by law.

Third-party services

EnString relies on a few providers, each with their own privacy practices: Supabase (accounts and database), Google (optional sign-in), and GitHub (hosting). Signing in with Google shares only your name, email, and profile picture with EnString.

Data retention

We keep your account information and board content for as long as your account exists. You can delete individual boards at any time from within the app. When you ask us to delete your account, we remove your account and its associated content; residual copies may persist briefly in routine backups before being overwritten.

Your choices and rights

  • Access. You can view all of your content directly in the app whenever you're signed in.
  • Export. You can export any board to a JSON file from within the app at any time.
  • Delete. You can delete individual boards in the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at jae3njae@gmail.com and we will remove it.
  • Correct. You can edit your content at any time, and you can update your account email or name through your sign-in provider.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights over your personal data — for example, under the EU/UK GDPR (access, correction, deletion, portability, and objection) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We honor these requests regardless of where you are. Contact us at jae3njae@gmail.com to exercise them, and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

International users

EnString is operated from the United States, and your data is stored on U.S.-based servers. If you use the app from outside the United States, you understand that your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

Children

EnString is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at jae3njae@gmail.com and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page, and where appropriate we will provide additional notice.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email jae3njae@gmail.com.
